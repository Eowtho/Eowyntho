How Did it Come to be?

Main Page

I had grown up always loving tea, so much so that I came out the delivery room with a mug in hand. Ever since I could remember, a nice hot cup of tea was always a part of my morning or night routine. As I grew up, ofcourse my palette had become more refined, wanting to explore more teas on my own. I'll always remember the shock seeing how much it was for just 2 ounces of tea, let alone the shipping! Since then, I made it my life goal to be able to make affordable teas online for everyone to enjoy. My teas are sourced both locally in my own home, as well as from my own friends overseas for you to enjoy. Take a browse through my selections and select and old favorite, or experiment with somethng new!
